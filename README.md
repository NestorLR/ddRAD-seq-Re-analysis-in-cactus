# ddRAD-seq-Re-analysis-in-cactus
This repository document the re-analysis for ddRAD-seq data from the phylogenetic study of [**Böhnert *et al.*** (2025)]( https://doi.org/10.1002/tax.13311): **Systematic and taxonomic revision of the genus *Austrocactus* (Cactaceae) based on morphology and genome wide SNP-data** and [**Merklinger*et al.*** (2021)](https://doi.org/10.1002/ajb2.1608): **Quaternary diversification of a columnar cactus in the driest place on earth**

ddRAD sequence reads were obtanined at the European Nucleotide Archive (ENA)[https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/home] under the BioProjects PRJEB79940 and PRJEB39114.
